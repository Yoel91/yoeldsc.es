RESTORE THE LIGHT

Mapu, a nature spirit, seems to be the last of his kind. After years of solitude, she has lost the light of life, the power she received when she was born. As the shadows prey everything, The Broken Forests needs more than ever the protection of the nature spirits, the Ra'qi.

When Mapu had lost her will, Lokko, a small firefly, offers his help, lending the last Ra'qi the little light he has. They will have to work together, to recover Mapu's powers, defend the Broken Forests and restore the light.

THE GAME

Mapu and the lost light is a 3D platformer, in wich the player takes the role of Mapu and Lokko in their quest to save The Broken Forests from an unknonw and powerful foe. Taking inspiration from platform titles from N64 and PSX era, Mapu and the lost light will inmerse the players in a powerful experience, a story they will remember.

Developed in Unreal Engine, the title is aimed to reach PC market with no determined launch date.

THE BROKEN FORESTS

The Broken Forests are a group of islands floating in the space. Once it was full of life, but with the lost of the Ra'qi and their light of life, every live being is being threatened by the shadows, making the Broken Forests an uninhabited wasteland.

Mapu and Lokko must explore the many different islands, restoring the connections between them by making the Ra'qi Life Flowers bloom again, casting the shadows away and finding what causes them.
